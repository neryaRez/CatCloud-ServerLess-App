Serverless Resources

Lambda Scanner

Lists all S3 objects and prepares execution details.

SNS Topic

Sends email notification after every Lambda execution.

Manual Test

Invoke Lambda

aws lambda invoke \
  --function-name <LambdaFunctionName> \
  --cli-binary-format raw-in-base64-out \
  --payload file://test-events/manual-test-event.json \
  response.json

cat response.json

After invocation, check the configured email inbox for the SNS notification. The SNS email subscription must be confirmed after first deployment.
